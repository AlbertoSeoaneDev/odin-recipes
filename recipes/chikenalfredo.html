How to cook a delicious Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy and comforting Italian-American pasta dish made with fettuccine pasta, a rich Alfredo sauce, and tender pieces of chicken. It's a perfect meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • For the Chicken Alfredo:
    • 200 grams fettuccine pasta
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
    1. Cook the Fettuccine:
      1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
      2. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente.
      3. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and then drain the fettuccine.
    2. Cook the Chicken:
      1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
      2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
      3. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
      4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest before slicing it into strips.
    3. Prepare the Alfredo Sauce:
      1. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
      2. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer.
      3. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
      4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    4. Combine and Serve:
      1. Add the cooked fettuccine to the Alfredo sauce and toss to combine.
      2. Add pasta water as needed to reach the desired consistency.
      3. Top with sliced chicken and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
      4. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo!
