How to cook a delicious lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is enjoyed worldwide. It consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce, all baked together to create a hearty and flavorful meal. This dish is perfect for family gatherings and special occasions due to its rich taste and satisfying nature.

Ingredients

  • For the meat sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 500 grams ground beef or Italian sausage
    • 800 grams canned crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the béchamel sauce:
    • 4 tublespoons butter
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 4 cups milk
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For assembling:
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 500 grams ricotta cheese
    • 42 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
    1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
      1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
      2. Cook chopped onion until translucent, about 5 minutes.
      3. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
      4. Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned.
      5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and red wine (if using).
      6. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
      7. Simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    2. Make the Béchamel Sauce:
      1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
      2. Add flour and whisk continuously for about 2 minutes.
      3. Gradually add milk while whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
      4. Cook and whisk until the sauce thickens and starts to bubble.
      5. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat.
    3. Assemble the Lasagna:
      1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
      2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
      3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
      4. Spread a layer of meat sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce.
      5. Spoon dollops of ricotta cheese over the béchamel, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
      6. Repeat the layers (noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan) until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of béchamel and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    4. Bake the Lasagna:
      1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (lightly oil the underside of the foil to prevent sticking).
      2. Bake for 25 minutes.
      3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
      4. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
    5. Serve:
      1. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
      2. Slice and serve hot.
